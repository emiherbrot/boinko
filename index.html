About

We plant to sail further into the Arctic that has been reached before du to retreating sea ice, in the Greenland sea. Along the way we will monitor oceanographic changes and document our jounrey to bring to attention the severe loss of sea ice, and what it means for our world.

The expedition started as part of a special research group at Clare Hall, Cambridge, of which Boinko the penguin is the mascot. People from diverse backgrounds met up to discuss climate change and polar issues
