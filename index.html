About

The expedition started as part of a special research group at Clare Hall, Cambridge. People from diverse backgrounds met up to discuss climate change and polar issues

Our goal is to reach further into the Arctic that has been reached in the Greenland sea, by sail. We will take oceanobgraphic mesaurements and document our jounrey to bring to attention the unprecedented loss of sea ice in the Arcitc, and what that means for our world.
